## Agent News
### Introduction
This note provides a summary of the latest news and developments in AI agents, with a focus on agentic AI, AI agents in finance, and the importance of trust and security in AI agents.
### Findings
* AI agents are becoming increasingly prevalent in various industries, including finance, with BNY Mellon launching an initiative to deploy 20,000 AI agents across its global workforce to enhance institutional AI literacy and operational efficiency (Source: StartupHub.ai).
* Researchers at MIT have developed an AI-powered agent that can turn 2D sketches into 3D CAD models, lowering the barrier to 3D modeling for designers and engineers (Source: MIT News).
* Agentic AI is becoming a key trend in AI, with AI agents acting more like teammates than tools and playing a bigger role in daily work (Source: Microsoft Source).
* Building trust in AI agents is essential, with security being a top priority to ensure agents don’t turn into “double agents” carrying unchecked risk (Source: Microsoft Source).
### Conclusion
The latest news and developments in AI agents highlight the increasing prevalence of AI agents in various industries, the importance of trust and security in AI agents, and the potential of agentic AI to revolutionize the way we work.
